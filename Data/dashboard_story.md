# Dashboard Story

## Executive Summary

The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales, profitability, customer behavior, shipping performance, and return patterns.

## What is Performing Well

High-performing regions and profitable product categories are driving overall revenue growth.

## What is Underperforming

Certain categories generate lower profitability and show higher return rates.

## Risks

Excessive discounting may negatively affect profit margins.

## Opportunities

Expanding successful product categories and improving regional performance can increase revenue.

## Recommended Actions

Optimize discount strategy, reduce return-prone products, and improve shipping efficiency.

## Limitations

The dashboard shows historical trends and does not establish causation.

## Next Analysis

Conduct deeper customer and product-level profitability analysis.
